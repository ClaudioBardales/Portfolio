About Me

I got into programming back in peru when i was 5 years old. I would mess around with the command prompt, and one time i accidentally deleted system32 when i was 11. Ever since then it has been my dream to become a developer, and my dreams came clear when the new first iphone got announced back when i was in my freshman year in high school. From that point i just wanted to create applications and websites for companies and see my work come to fruition.

I'm focused on Front-End technologies, but i am also intersted in back-end development, and machine learning. When im not programming I usually play basketball, play fighting games, and sing in japanese.
