Claudio Bardales

<Web Developer />

I got into programming back in Peru when I was 5 years old. I would mess around with the command prompt, and one time I accidentally deleted system32 when I was 11. Ever since then it has been my dream to become a developer, and my dreams came clear when the new first iPhone got announced back when I was in my freshman year in high school. From that point, I just wanted to create applications and websites for companies and see my work come to fruition. I'm focused on Front-End technologies, but I am also interested in back-end development, and machine learning. When im not programming I usually play basketball, play fighting games, and sing in Japanese.
